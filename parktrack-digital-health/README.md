# ParkTrack – Digital Health Research Prototype

ParkTrack is a digital health prototype developed to support Parkinson’s disease research through structured self-monitoring and symptom tracking.

The project was developed as part of a Bachelor project in Biomedical Engineering at DTU in collaboration with healthcare professionals and Parkinson’s disease stakeholders.

## Project Focus

* Digital health technologies
* Patient-centered design
* Healthcare data collection
* Symptom tracking and visualization
* Research-oriented healthcare workflows
* UX and interdisciplinary collaboration

## Features

* Medication reminders and logging
* Symptom monitoring
* Tremor and movement tracking
* Sleep and heart-rate monitoring
* Research data visualization
* Research-oriented export functionality

## Development Process

The project followed a user-centered and interdisciplinary design process involving:

* Parkinson’s disease patients
* Relatives
* Healthcare professionals
* Clinical stakeholders
* Researchers

The prototype was designed and developed in Figma as a research-oriented healthcare application concept.
